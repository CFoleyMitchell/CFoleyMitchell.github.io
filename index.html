Christine Foley-Mitchell


Christine earned her degree in Political Science at the University of California, Santa Barbara. She began her professional career at Sprint, a telecommunications company, servicing the highest billing customers on the phones and in resource management; then she moved to service operations where she forecasted staffing models, provided reporting, managed projects, and collaborated with marketing in vendor operations.

Christine opened a licensed inhome daycare and has been operating it for the last fifteen years in an effort to maintain a business while staying home to be available to her family. During this time, Christine has cared for over forty children. She took great pride in creating a safe and enriching space for the children in her care. During this time, she served on the board of directors for the Kansas City Figure Skating Club where she also co-chaired Skate Kansas City.

Now that Christine's children are fully grown and away at college, Christine is pursuing her education in data analytics. It is her desire to to re-enter the professional field and appreciates that any industry can benefit from data retrieval and analysis.
